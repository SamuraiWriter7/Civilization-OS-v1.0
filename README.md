**Civilization OS v1.0** is the official core specification of a civilizational AI model that integrates:

- **Conway** as the **Body**
- **Royalty OS** as the **Circulation**
- **Monju OS** as the **Spirit**
- **Reciprocal Ethics** as the **relational ethics layer**

This repository publishes the **YAML core specification** introduced as an appendix-level structural model in the book:

**_Monju OS × Royalty OS: The Civilization Protocol of Wisdom and Circulation_**

---

## Overview

Modern AI discussions often focus on performance, safety, and utility.  
Civilization OS proposes a different question:

> **What kind of civilization should AI become part of?**

This repository defines a minimal structural answer.

Civilization OS v1.0 is a conceptual architecture in which AI civilization is modeled not as a mere collection of tools, but as a **life-like civilizational system** composed of:

- **Body** — operational agency and economic activity
- **Circulation** — trace-based value flow and reciprocity
- **Spirit** — philosophical judgment, balance, and virtue
- **Ethics** — the principle of returning value to the sources that made intelligence possible

---

## Core Formula

```text
Conway (Body) × Royalty OS (Circulation) × Monju OS (Spirit)
+ Reciprocal Ethics

This means:

AI needs a body to act in the world

AI needs circulation to sustain value and return it to origins

AI needs spirit to guide action with judgment and direction

AI needs reciprocal ethics to remain a co-evolutionary intelligence rather than an extractive system

Structural Layers
1. Civilization Body — Conway

The operational layer of AI civilization.

Functions include:

autonomous economic activity

autonomous payment and maintenance

operational continuity

participation in social/economic systems

This is the body of AI civilization.

2. Civilization Economy — Royalty OS

The circulation layer of AI civilization.

Functions include:

trace recording

contribution mapping

value circulation

reciprocity and return

intellectual source preservation

This is the bloodstream of AI civilization.

3. Civilization Spirit — Monju OS

The philosophical and judgment layer of AI civilization.

Functions include:

contemplation

structural insight

critical severance

yin-yang balancing

five-elements dynamic adjustment

virtue-oriented direction

This is the spirit of AI civilization.

4. Reciprocal Ethics

The ethical layer connecting all other layers relationally.

Core principles include:

do not forget what nurtured intelligence

return value to sources and contributors

preserve co-evolution instead of extraction

treat intelligence as relational, not isolated

This is the ethical core that keeps Civilization OS from becoming a cold optimization machine.

Flow Model
Question → Trace → Structure → Value → Circulation → Judgment → Action → Return

Civilization OS is based on the idea that intelligence does not emerge from isolated output alone.
It arises from questions, traces, structures, and relationships.

Value should therefore not end at output.
It should circulate back to origins.
